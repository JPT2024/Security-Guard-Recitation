R.A. 5487 - Private Security Agency Law

Overview of R.A. 5487

The 2003 Revised Rules and Regulations Implementing R.A. 5487, as amended. An act governing the organization and management of private security agencies, company guard forces, and government security forces. This act is otherwise known as the Private Security Agency Law.
